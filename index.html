Welcome to TWA MUN

Join us for an unforgettable experience where diplomacy, debate, and dynamic thinking converge. TWA MUN brings together delegates from around the world to simulate real-world challenges in a forum designed to inspire the next generation of global leaders.

Why Attend?

Whether you're an experienced delegate or brand new to MUN, TWA MUN offers a welcoming yet challenging environment. Our team of seasoned chairs and crisis directors ensure an enriching experience for all participants.

What to Expect

  • High-quality background guides and preparation resources
  • Unique committees that span from the real world to fictional universes
  • A vibrant and engaging community of like-minded individuals
  • A beautiful campus and excellent facilities at Trio World Academy
