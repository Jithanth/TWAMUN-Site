About TWA MUN

TWA MUN is an annual Model United Nations conference hosted by The Whitefield Academy. Our mission is to foster global citizenship and leadership through debate, collaboration, and diplomacy.

Founded in 2019, TWA MUN has grown into a prestigious platform for young minds to challenge world issues and propose innovative solutions.
