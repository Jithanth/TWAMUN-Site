United Nations Security Council (UNSC)

Agenda: Equitable and sustainable distribution of Middle Eastern oil resources.

The UNSC will delve into the geopolitics and economic imperatives surrounding oil in the Middle East. Delegates will confront the challenge of equitable access while ensuring global stability and compliance with international law.

International Court of Justice (ICJ)

Agenda: Assigning responsibility for climate change and environmental degradation.

The ICJ committee will debate the legal and moral responsibilities of nations contributing to climate change. Delegates will explore precedent and propose solutions for international legal accountability.

United States Senate

Agenda: Examining the role of tariffs in contemporary American economic policy.
